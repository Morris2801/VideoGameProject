Story

Welcome to Mayaztec, a thrilling journey through a mystical pyramid based in Aztec and Mayan mythology. If you unfourtunatley die, you will keep your most recent card that you picked up and will be able to use it in your next life whenever you please. Your goal is to defeat the gods that are waiting for you at the end, so, will you be the one to break the curse and defeat the gods, or will you be just another victim? Down below, you will find the cards you will encounter in the game, use them wisely

How to Play

Its really simple really, you use "WASD" to move press "X" to attack the many enemies you will face, "F" to interact with the vases and cards, and use the numbers 1 through 6 to activate the cards you have, 1 being the left-most card and 6 being the right-most card.

Enemies

You will encounter many enemies in your journey trying to keep you in the curse, some of them are:

    Tlaxcalteca, Mariachi

    Warrior, Devil

    Bosses Quetzalcoatl and Ah Puch

Card Properties

Each card has its own unique properties that will help you break the curse, you have transformation, melee, and power-up cards, in that order below:

Transform to temporarly gain +15 health and +5 damage

Transform to temporarly gain +2 stamina

Transform to temporarly gain +10 health

Most damage buff at +6, but slightly harder to find

Weakest of the 3 at +4 damage, great at the early game

More damage than the knife at +5, weaker than the machete

Gain +10 temporary health, great when low on health

Gain +10 permanent health, great when comming out of a battle

Damage increased +10 for a short period of time
